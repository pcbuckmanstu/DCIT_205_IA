Courses we offer

Our eight week intensive certificate course is open to senior high school graduates, tertiary students, school graduates, workers and the general public.

Introduction to Computer Science

Overview of fundamental concepts in computer science. Introduces programming and problem-solving techniques.

Data Structures and Algorithms

Study of essential data structures (arrays, linked lists, trees) and algorithms. Emphasis on efficiency and best practices in algorithm design

Computer Networks

Exploration of the principles and protocols governing computer networks. Topics include TCP/IP, routing, and network security.

Database Management Systems

Introduction to database concepts and design. Hands-on experience with popular database management systems.

Web Development

Covers front-end and back-end web development technologies. Emphasis on creating dynamic and interactive web applications.

Artificial Intelligence

Overview of AI concepts, including machine learning and natural language processing. Practical applications in problem-solving and decision-making.
